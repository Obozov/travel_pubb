FAQ

What is the basis for the profit of the company and investors of the project?

The project was created to monetize capital! What does this mean?

Not everyone can earn money on currency conversions, capitalization indices, stock transactions, stock exchanges, bitcoin, crypto-currency mining, start-ups and so on.

The company's specialists track the most successful companies: they check and analyze their potential, work experience and investment growth - and only after that they decide to invest (or, conversely, refuse) in this kind of resources. With revenues from investing in successful projects, the company earns and generates accruals to investors.
